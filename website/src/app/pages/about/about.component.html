Description

DiaMetrics is an Angular web application meant to improve diabetes healthcare outcomes. DiaMetrics takes the data from a diabetic's life and plots the glucose levels against time throughout the day. It lists only the past 24 hours worth of data as to only contain relevant information. Calculations are also done based on the food consumed, as the ratio of the carbohydrates and insulin are exponentially weighted through time in a moving average. This average weights relevant information more and depicts a proper insulin to glucose level ratio.

Motivation

The primary motivation behind this was that one of our group members is afflicted with type 1 diabetes. Trying to make an web application to ease the viewing of blood sugar data only came naturally after. With the numpy Python library, we were also able to perform exponential weighted average calculations over the glucose to insulin ratio. This statistic monitors variations above the norm and will ideally be a safety net as it is predictive of trend changes. Easing the trouble of monitoring diabetes was simply personal to us.
